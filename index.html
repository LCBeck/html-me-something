My Hawaii Vacation

My family went on a really great vacation to Hawaii.

We stayed at the Sheraton Waikiki. It was a beautiful hotel at the beach with a nice view of Diamond Head.

We went to a Luau while we were there. That honestly was amazing and I'm glad we did it.

The Hula is the heartbeat of the Hawaiian People.

We snorkeled in Hananama Bay. There are colorful fish every everywhere in the water.

We went to Volcanos National Park to see lava rock that has cooled and formed over roads, trees and plants.

Those are definitely some things you should try if you go to Hawaii.
