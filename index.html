My Hawaii Vacation

My family went on a really great vacation to Hawaii when I was 14 years old.

Hawaii



We stayed at the Sheraton Waikiki. In the picture below you can see how it is really

close to the beach. It was a beautiful hotel with a nice view of Diamond Head.

Sheraton Waikiki Resort



We went to a Luau while we were there. You can see in the picture below some of the hula dancers.

That honestly was amazing and I'm glad we went to it.

Luau

The Hula is the heartbeat of the Hawaiian People.



Hanauma Bay is the next picture. We went there specifically to snorkel. There are colorful fish everywhere in the water.

It's in a bay so there are really no sharks so it feels pretty safe.

Hanauma Bay



In the picture below you can see we went to Volcanos National Park to see lava rock that has cooled and formed over roads, trees and plants.

These are my cousins, Lyla and Adrian, in the picture below and they came with us on our vacation.

Family



I feel like we were very lucky to visit Hawaii. These are definitely some places you

should visit if you are able to go. The weather is beautiful and so many memories can be made.
